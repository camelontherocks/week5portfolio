Boating is, of course, very popular in the San Juans. There're many types of boats out there for a variety of uses; fishing, crabbing, shrimping, sailing, cruising, and just getting from point A to point B. Some things to consider, in alignment with what the boat will be used for, are of course size (which affects weight, portability, engine power required, and maintenance costs), but also what kind of equipment; radar, autoiplot, electronic charts, navigation, etc..

One popular boat often seen in the San Juans is an Explorer manfactured by Sea Sport. It's One example of The Cormorant can sleep two, and has a refrigerator, stove, sink, dinette, and of course a head (toilet). We can live on this boat for days!!!

About The SeaSport Explorer

  • Local - Made in Bellingham, right here in the Pacific N.W..
  • Capable - Long range with two 60 gallon tanks.
  • Solid - Very solid/heavy construction.
  • Versatile - You can fish, crab, shrimp, or just cruise.
  • Common - Plenty of knowledge and support around these boats.
