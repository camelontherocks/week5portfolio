The main San Juan islands... in order of size...

  • San Juan - The largest and most populated.
  • Orcas - Not much meat on those bones.
  • Lopez - Least populated of the big three.
  • Shaw - Smallest served by state ferry service.
  • Decatur - Not served by state ferry.

The islands each have their own character. You can read more about them at: San Juan Islands Website
